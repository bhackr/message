How Binary Text Conversion Works

This visualization shows the relationship between characters and their binary representation.

  • Character to Binary: Each character is converted to its ASCII/Unicode value, then to 8-bit binary
  • Hashtags: Words starting with # are preserved as-is without conversion
  • Spaces: Spaces between binary groups are visual separators only
  • UTF-8: Special characters use multi-byte sequences in UTF-8 encoding
